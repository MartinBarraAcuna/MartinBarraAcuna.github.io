About me

I am deeply interested in the philosophy of economics, particularly from the perspective of the philosophy of science, as well as the relationship between economics, political philosophy, and moral philosophy. My undergraduate thesis focuses on the concept of welfare in microeconomic theory, exploring the role of non-epistemic values in science and the connection between scientific theories and political decision-making.

Works

Now I am currently working in:

  • Formal Toolbox for Philosophy Students. A toolbox of resources for philosophy students, designed to formalize they ideas. Based on the Open Logic Project, it provides an accessible introduction to formal tools for engaging with contemporary philosophical debates.

  • Para todo x: Santiago (translation of Forall x: Calgary ). It is a comprehensive textbook on formal logic, covering everything from basic concepts to advanced topics, with exercises and solutions. It is available in multiple formats (PDF, HTML, LaTeX) and is freely accessible. This Spanish translation, done by the Chilean Association of Analytical Philosophy Students (AChEFA), seeks to spread current philosophical knowledge in Spanish, maintaining fidelity to the original with only minor aesthetic modifications. The book can be downloaded on Github and the AChEFA website and its code in GitHub.

  • Biografías de lógicos y lógicas (translation of the appendix of biographies of the Open Logic Project ). The book can be downloaded on GitHub and the AChEFA website.

  • An article with Carlos Romero on ontic structural realism and the case of microeconomics as a case for constraint realism

  • An Open Introduction to Game Theory for Philosophy Students. This text tries to generate an open introduction to game theory in Spanish, focused on philosophy students. It is intended to emphasize how this tool can shed light on problems of coordination, cooperation and morality. A draft in progress can be viewed here. When the book is in a more advanced stage I will publish the code in github
